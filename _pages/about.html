About me


I'm Yanhui ZHANG, Member of Chinese Association of Automation, now as PhD at Zhejiang University majoring in Aeronautical and Astronautical Science and Technology, interest in Hypersonic Aircraft design and navigation control, and Robots navigation control and MotionPlanning.

From 2014 to 2017, I am studying for a master's degree in School of Automation, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA).

From 2017 to 2020, I have been working in iFLYTEK for the research of image algorithm and autonomous driving algorithm.

From 2024 to 2025, I am Visiting National University of Singapore (NUS), funding by China Scholarship of Council (CSC) for the research of Advance Learning Control.

From 2021 to Present, I am pursuiting my Ph.D degree in Zhejiang University (ZJU) for the research of control and motionplanning of UAV/Self-driving cars/Unpowered sailing vessel and hypersonic aircraft.
