About me


I'm Yanhui ZHANG, Member of Chinese Association of Automation, now a Research Assistant at Zhejiang University majoring in Aerospace Science and Technology engineering, interest in robotics control, navigation, planning systems and optimization.

From 2014 to 2017, I am studying for a master's degree in School of Automation, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA).

From 2017 to 2020, I have been working in iFLYTEK for the research of image algorithm and autonomous driving algorithm.

From 2020 to Present, I am working in Zhejiang University for the research of control and motionplanning of UAV/Self-driving cars/Unpowered sailing vessel and hypersonic aircraft.
